About KCA

Promoting and sharing Korean culture within the MIT community since 1990

The MIT Korean Cultural Association (KCA) is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting Korean culture and creating a supportive community for Korean and Korean-American students at MIT, as well as anyone interested in Korean culture.


Our mission is to promote cultural awareness and appreciation through various events, activities, and educational opportunities. We organize cultural festivals, language exchange programs, traditional performances, and social gatherings throughout the academic year.


KCA serves as a bridge between Korean and American cultures, fostering intercultural understanding and friendship among MIT students from diverse backgrounds.
